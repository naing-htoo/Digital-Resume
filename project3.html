International Language & Business Center

  • Case Study

"Building a robust IT backbone for educational excellence."

  • Deployed WiFi and network infrastructure using EnGenius Access Points and Switches, as well as MikroTik Routers, across multiple branches including Yangon International School, Mandalay Branch.
  • Designed and implemented scalable network architectures, ensuring reliable and high-performance connectivity for faculty, staff, and students at both locations.
  • Conducted site surveys and performed network optimization to ensure optimal coverage and signal strength in the school environments.
  • Implemented network security measures, including authentication protocols and firewall rules, to safeguard network integrity and protect against unauthorized access.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams to meet the unique IT requirements of both ILBC International School and Yangon International School, Mandalay Branch.
