"KALAW-NET Internet Service Provider"

  • Orchestrated the successful transition from a Wireless Internet Service Provider (WISP) to Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH) infrastructure, leading the migration of the access network using ZTE OLT and radius servers, and multiple upstreams for redundant connections.
  • Managed the operations of the ISP, overseeing a team of technicians and providing comprehensive training, transforming them into proficient fiber technicians.
  • Demonstrated exceptional leadership in managing the construction of the Optical Distribution Network (ODN) around Kalaw, including overhead fiber cabling, core planning, and ensuring efficient network connectivity.
  • Led the deployment and optimization of EnGenius, MikroTik, TP-Link, and Cambium Networks Wireless Backhauls, ensuring reliable connectivity for wireless subscribers.
  • Successfully negotiated with local authorities and other stakeholders to secure necessary permits, rights-of-way, and agreements, facilitating the smooth deployment and expansion of the network infrastructure.
  • Implemented efficient purchasing practices, acquiring essential items such as fiber optic cables, ODN equipment, and network devices from local and foreign suppliers, while building and maintaining strong relationships with vendors.
  • Managed the assets of the ISP, including network infrastructure, equipment, and other company assets, implementing effective asset management strategies and conducting regular audits.
  • Developed and maintained a mini Network Operation Center (NOC) for the access network, utilizing tools such as SmartOLT and the Dude for network monitoring and management.
  • Fostered a collaborative work environment, encouraging team members' professional growth and ensuring high levels of performance.
