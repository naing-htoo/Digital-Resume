International Language & Business Center, Thuwana Campus

  • Case Study

"Empowering learning through cutting-edge technology and seamless connectivity."

  • Spearheaded a comprehensive IT infrastructure project at ILBC International School, Thuwana Branch, overseeing the deployment of a robust 3-tier network architecture. This included the utilization of EnGenius Switches and Access Points, as well as a MikroTik High-End Router and Aggregate Switches.
  • Implemented cutting-edge technologies to support a large campus with 2000 to 3000 daily network clients, ensuring seamless and reliable network connectivity throughout the premises.
  • Played a key role in designing and managing the network infrastructure, which featured redundant fiber cores and dual home up-links to prevent physical failures and enhance network resilience.
  • Supported various IT functions, including software installations, system upgrades, and data backup and recovery, ensuring data security and continuity.
  • Demonstrated expertise in deploying advanced security measures, including a Fortinet firewall with Threat Protection, Application Controls, and Intrusion Prevention System, safeguarding the network from potential threats.
  • Collaborated closely with cross-functional teams to meet the unique IT requirements of the school, optimizing network performance and providing uninterrupted access to critical resources.
