Mandalay Waterboom & Mandalay Bay Resort

  • Case Study

"Crafting a technologically advanced ecosystem for unparalleled guest experiences."

  • Led the deployment of over 100 IP security cameras using Dahua CCTV Systems, implementing a comprehensive surveillance system for enhanced security and monitoring.
  • Designed and deployed a redundant fiber ring network using EnGenius Switches, ensuring reliable and high-speed connectivity across the 8-acre resort.
  • Implemented Mikrotik Routers, EnGenius Wireless Access Points and Switches, optimizing WiFi coverage and network performance for staff and guests.
  • Integrated Infosat IPTV Systems, enabling seamless streaming of multimedia content throughout the resort.
  • Deployed Yealink and Yeastar for IPPBX Systems, providing efficient and cost-effective communication solutions for staff and guests.
  • Implemented a video wall system for effective display and management of visual content.
